Maybe Types

A Maybe-Type
  • is a Sum-Type
  • one of whose addend-types is Null-Type

Error or Nothing
1 let MaybeE = Type (Error, null);
2 x (null , MaybeE);
3 x (new Error(), MaybeE);
4 not ( is (0 , MaybeE));
 

 

x() converts its arguments after first into a Type,
you don't have to call Type() explicitly:

x() -shorthand:
1 x (null, Error, null);
2 x (new Error(), Error, null);
